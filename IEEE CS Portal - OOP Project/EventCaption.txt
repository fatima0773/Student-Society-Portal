Attention all the data enthusiasts, data designers, and data developers, this is your time to showcase your creativity and talent in the first-ever Data Hub of the banking industry!   IEEE Computer Society CUI, in collaboration with United Bank Limited (UBL) Pakistan, brings you an informative session to guide you about the first-ever Datathon by UBL.   With a sole motto – to Innovate – “UBL Datathon’21”, the students are provided an opportunity to participate in the competition and interact with the representatives of the top-notch organizations. The shortlisted teams will have a chance to present their ideas in the “Datathon Symposium 2021”.
